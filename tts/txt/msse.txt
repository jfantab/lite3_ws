The Master of Science in Software Engineering (MSSE) program at San José State University (SJSU) is a premier graduate offering designed to prepare students for success in the global software industry, with a focus on large-scale design, component integration, and quality assurance. 
Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, the program offers six specializations—Enterprise Software Technologies, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Software Systems Engineering, Networking Software, and Cybersecurity—and emphasizes hands-on learning through state-of-the-art labs and industry collaboration. 
Students complete 33 units, including core courses, specialization electives, and a culminating project or thesis. With its strong industry ties, practical curriculum, and commitment to lifelong learning, the MSSE program equips graduates with technical expertise, leadership skills, and a deep understanding of ethical and global impacts in software engineering.
